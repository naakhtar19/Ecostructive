Why we need Recycling?

Recycling is an activity that saves energy, cuts back on the pollution we create and most importantly it conserves our natural resources. By recycling we are taking the first step towards a sustainable environment. We are also putting the planet first and doing our part for preserving these for our future generations.

It is recommended that we follow the policy of reducing the amount of waste produced, reusing the materials repeatedly and recycling it to make new products.

Reduce. Reuse. Recycle.

Reduce, Reuse, Recycle. These three words are frequently used together when we think about adopting a sustainable way to live. Not all people have the proper access to resources that allow efficient waste management, which sometimes leads to hoarding, or unnecessary collection of items at home.

Hoarding items takes up a lot of space that could have been used for a better purpose. Some may argue that hoarding is more of a mental disorder rather than a physical one but it also has to do with the fact that not many people know the proper way to get rid of stuff.
