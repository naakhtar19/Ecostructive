About Us

Save time on getting rid of the waste in your house by signing up at Ecostructive!

We have observed that a lot of people in the community are ignorant towards the disposal of waste. A common man would throw his trash out rather than sorting it into different categories of waste. In order to bridge this gap Ecostructive provides the customer a platform to place requests for scheduled waste pickup and a tracking mechanism to ensure it's being recycled properly.
